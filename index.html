About Us

Dependable Engineering Professionals of Namibia (DEPN)

Dependable Engineering Professionals of Namibia (DEPN) is a registered non-profit organization that ought to be a key strategy to help Junior Engineers and other graduates that suit our profile to be a part of Mega engineering projects within the country.

Read More

History

The idea of starting the organization came after realizing that there are soo many brilliant graduates on the street that are never given the opportunity to exercise their abilities and skills

Objective

DEPN ought to render our services through doing feasibility studies and research, Project management, project designs and EIAs. All our services are free of charge.

Mission

The mandate of DEPN is to serve the national interest through research, development, technology transfer, to promote mineral technology as well helping foster the establishment and expansion of industries.

Vision

To be the body that facilitates mining and marine, value addition, Recycling and storage, and Oil and Gas, projects that may potentially result in job creation and industry growth.
